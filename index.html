Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

How fast is fiber-optic internet compared to cable?

+

Fiber-optic internet is significantly faster than traditional cable. While cable internet typically maxes out at around 100-200 Mbps, our fiber network delivers speeds up to 1 Gbps (1000 Mbps) with symmetrical upload and download speeds.

Is there a data cap on FibeFly plans?

+

No, all FibeFly plans come with unlimited data. You can stream, download, and browse as much as you want without worrying about hitting a data limit or incurring additional charges.

How long does installation take?

+

Professional installation typically takes 2-4 hours. Our certified technicians will handle everything from running the fiber line to setting up your equipment and ensuring optimal performance.

What equipment do I need?

+

We provide all necessary equipment including a fiber-optic modem and Wi-Fi router. The equipment is included in your monthly plan at no additional cost, and we handle all maintenance and updates.

Can I keep my current phone number?

+

Yes, you can port your existing phone number to our service. We'll handle the transfer process to ensure there's no interruption in your phone service.
